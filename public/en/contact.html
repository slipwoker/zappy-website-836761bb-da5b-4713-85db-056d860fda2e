    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I try rackets before purchasing?

    Absolutely! We encourage customers to try different rackets before purchasing. We have a variety of demo rackets available, and we can arrange on-court testing if needed.

    How long does stringing service take?

    Our regular stringing service takes 24-48 hours. We also offer express service that takes up to 4 hours for urgent cases (subject to availability).

    How do I know which racket is suitable for my child?

    Our expert team will help you choose the right racket according to the child's age, height and skill level. We recommend coming to the store with the child for accurate measurement and fitting.

    Is there a warranty on the equipment?

    All our products come with full manufacturer warranty. Additionally, we provide technical support and service throughout the product's lifetime.

    Do you offer discounts for groups or clubs?

    Yes, we offer special prices for group purchases, clubs and tennis schools. Contact us for more details.

    What is the return and exchange policy?

    We allow returns or exchanges of unopened products within 14 days of purchase. Opened or used products can be exchanged subject to certain conditions.
